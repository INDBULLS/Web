About me

I'm INDBULLS and We're a

IND BULLS was found in March, 2020 with an objective to make game-enthusiasts aware of the opportunities that eSports provides to reach highly engaged audience.
Our main motive is to promote and develop eSports as a main stream sports ensuring equal access while instilling values of teamwork, respect, discipline and integrity.
We are giving our best to provide great platform to the PRO teams as well as the UNDERDOGS and contribute to the ultimate growth of Indian Gaming Community.
We hereby bring opportunity and exposure to all the teams of IGC to show their skills and provide their worth in higher platforms.
